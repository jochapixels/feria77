Linde Material Handling

3D product film campaign

For the renowned manufacturer of forklift trucks, we developed tailor-made 3D films for their new top products. How can prototypes of new vehicles be put in the best possible light in action? Linde Material Handling, the world-renowned manufacturer of forklift trucks and warehouse technology equipment, decided on a 3D presentation.

We designed and animated several 3D films for the launch of the new combustion engine forklift H20-35 and the order picking truck N20. The strengths of the forklift truck were presented in individual film stories in the areas of Safety, Efficiency, Operating Experience and Hydrostatic Drive in a 3D external warehouse.

Another film summarized all highlights in dynamic action scenes. For the N20 order picker we created a complete 3D film in a warehouse. For both films, people were completely animated. The films were adapted in five languages and used internationally for various purposes.

Deutsche Telekom

Magenta Smart Home image campaign

The time is right for Smart Home! Authentic, approachable, direct - our video campaign about magenta Smart Home communicates state-of-the-art technology at eye level.

When people think of „smart home“, many people still think of tangled cables, complicated technology or even hackers. However, Telekom’s Magenta SmartHome proves that the technology has long been safe, mature and easy to use. The aim of its strategic image campaign is to overcome reservations. The aim was to show in an authentic way that the time is simply ripe for the smart home - for everyone, not just for the techies among us. The following three questions should be answered with the films: What is Smart Home anyway? What advantages does Magenta SmartHome offer? How does it work?

SSI Schäfer

Hololens Experience

Product stories as holograms - a visitor magnet for trade fairs. Through our HoloLens experience for SSI Schäfer, sophisticated packaging technologies are explained clearly and in detail.

For its trade fair appearance at the Logimat, SSI Schäfer, the world’s most powerful supplier of products and systems for internal material flow and waste management, needed a solution that would explain packaging products in a simple and entertaining way. With a HoloLens Experience, we made various functions and application possibilities tangible.

An example of this was the use on Märklin’s model railways, whose elaborately painted, sensitive individual parts required a unique container concept. As development partner of the agency 361/DRX GmbH, RECORDBAY took over the technical implementation of the HoloLens Experience. We implemented a storyline consisting of interactively selectable sequences with special image and video material.

Bayer AG

360° App

One app, all corporate worlds: A VR experience around Bayer AG with the Cardboard and an innovative app solution.

Bayer AG approached us to provide an app to accompany the Bayer event „Day One“, which received worldwide attention. We developed the concept of a 360°-degree app, which makes different parts of the group (Pharma, Animal Care, Consumer Health, Digital Crop Science) audiovisually experienceable for internal and external users.

In the technical implementation we developed a 2D and 3D design to meet the high visual requirements. On this basis, we then designed a complete Bayer Group world in 360°, in which we interweaved real image and 3D models.

Bombardier Transportation

Tangible Table Experience

Get sales conversations moving with digital installations. For our client Bombardier Transportation we developed an interactive exhibition stand with tangible table experience. We created exciting worlds in 3D.

To convince customers of future mobility concepts at trade fairs, our client Bombardier Transportation needed a permanent solution. For Bombardier’s global trade fair presence, we developed interactive digital installations and animated elaborate 3D worlds. Impressive, clear, sustainable - on tangible tables and interactive screens we digitally staged mobility solutions for urban areas of various sizes.

We recreated the complete traffic infrastructure in 3D models and animations, meticulously and with great attention to detail. Customers can have individual vehicle models such as trains presented in 3D by the experts and explained on the tables and screens. The focus was on simple operation.
